Gamma correction in OpenGL

  • Textures:
    • Use internal format GL_SRGB or GL_SRGB_ALPHA
    • Only use for color images (not normal map etc.)
    • Only on normalized integer types ignored for floating point

  • Framebuffer:

    • glEnable(GL_FRAMEBUFFER_SRGB)
    • Performs blending in linear space then changed to sRGB
    • Careful with multi objects

Bidirectional Reflectance Distribution Function

  • Function that:
    • Returns the ratio of light that gets reflected
    • At a specific point
    • Coming from one direction
    • And exiting on another direction
  • Determines the look of a material
  • Different models to approximate different materials
  • Can be obtained analitically (math formulas) \ measuring light response of a material
